Electrical Engineering Handbook

Author: Kirill Semenov

This is not a regular textbook. This 'book' is a dry compilation of the most usefull materials in electrical engineering from books and Internet. Use it as a handbook, where you can easily find equations, diagrams and ideas.

This books covers the most important topics that you need to know to become an Electrical Engineer.
